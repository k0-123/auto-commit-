INSTALLATION STEPS

1

EXTRACT RESOURCE

Locate the downloaded autocommit.zip file on your computer and extract it. You will get a folder containing the manifest and compiled scripts.

2

ENABLE DEV MODE

Open Google Chrome and navigate to the Extensions page: chrome://extensions/. In the top-right corner, toggle the Developer mode switch to ON.

3

LOAD EXTENSION

Click the Load unpacked button in the top-left corner, navigate to the folder where you extracted the files, and click select.

4

PIN TO TOOLBAR

Click the puzzle icon next to your profile picture in Chrome and click the Pin icon next to AutoCommit to keep it handy!
