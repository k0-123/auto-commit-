# Demo Video Script (≈60–90 seconds)

1. **0:00–0:10** — Open the toolbar popup. Show the "Hello" empty state turning
   into a populated task list. Narrate: "Plan and track tasks without leaving
   your browser."
2. **0:10–0:25** — Type in the quick-add box:
   `Finish report friday #work !high` and press Enter. Show the parsed
   priority, due date, and tag chip appear instantly.
3. **0:25–0:40** — Open the Focus tab. Start a Pomodoro session, show the timer
   ring and the live "today / pomodoros / streak" stats.
4. **0:40–0:55** — Right-click a web page, choose "Capture to Productivity
   Assistant", then open the Capture tab to show the imported item. Convert it
   to a task.
5. **0:55–1:10** — Open Insights. Show the completion trend chart, category
   donut, and activity heatmap. Click "Export CSV".
6. **1:10–1:20** — Open Settings, toggle dark mode and change the accent color
   to show instant theming. End on the logo.

Voiceover tone: calm, upbeat, concise. No background music needed.
